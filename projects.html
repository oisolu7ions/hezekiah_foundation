Flint Water Project

When the water crisis in Flint, Michigan, left families without safe drinking water, we knew we had to help. The Hezekiah Foundation launched the Flint Water Project to provide bottled water to affected communities.

Through the generosity of donors and the dedication of volunteers, we raised funds, secured donations, and coordinated delivery to those in need. On April 3rd, 2021, we delivered over 20,000 bottles of water across more than 500 cases, offering a measure of relief and hope to families who had been living with uncertainty.

This project was made possible by every person who gave their time, money, or voice. We are grateful to all who stood with the people of Flint.
